Welcome to Destination Hyrule's site.

Destination Hyrule, a provincial Crown corporation, leads the marketing of the popular Nintendo's game serires: The Legend of Zelda, Breath of the Wild, as a tourist destination and promotes the development and growth of the tourism industry.

Desination Hyrule is focused on attracting overnight vistors to the country, by promoting our natural brand to the world, and increasing tourism revenus by supporting industrie's local businesses in the development and delivery of remarkable experiences

We work collaboratively with Indigenous partners, industry, regional and community partners

In November 2020, Destination Hyrule launched its new strategy, designed to deliver strong, sustainable growth. This strategy defines a new path for working together with our Indigenous partners and community, to deliver and compete in one of the most competitive industries in the world.
