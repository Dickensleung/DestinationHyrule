About Us

Destination Hyrule supports a strong and competitive future for Hyrule’s tourism industry through a combination of global marketing, destination development, industry learning, cooperative community-based programs, and visitor servicing. Destination Hyrule’s programs help to improve the visitor experience, support businesses and communities across the province, and strengthen its worldwide reputation as a destination of choice.

Our strategy outlines how Destination Hyrule will reach its full potential as a tourism destination, and market the brand to the world.

Our Aspiration

Grow sustainable social, cultural, environmental and economic benefits for all citizens.

Our Strategy & Goal:

Revenue

Exceed 4% baseline and achieve target of 6% annual revenue growth for Tourism.

Visitors

Become the most highly recommended destination.

Residents

Grow sustainable social, cultural, environmental, and economic benefits for all citizens.

Industry

Build industry capacity through relevant programs and collaboration.
